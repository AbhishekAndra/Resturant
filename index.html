Our Story

Cooked with tradition, served with care

Stackly started as a small family kitchen with one goal: bring the honest, generous flavor of home-style Indian cooking to every doorstep. Every dish is made fresh to order using traditional techniques — no shortcuts, no compromises.

Our mission is simple — exceptional food, fair prices, and a delivery experience as considered as the meal itself.
